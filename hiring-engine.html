What LiHA Actually Does

Every step below marked AI is handled by the agent. Your recruiter owns the steps marked human — the decisions and relationships that actually matter.

Step 1
Define & Refine the Role AI
You provide goals and qualifications. LiHA converts them into optimized searches, suggests required vs. nice-to-have filters, and builds out the project automatically.
Step 2
Source & Shortlist AI
Runs dozens of searches across 1B+ members. Delivers a ranked shortlist with a plain-language explanation of why each candidate fits. Your recruiter reviews a curated list — not 300 raw results.
Step 3
Draft Outreach & Pre-Screen AI
Drafts personalized InMails at scale. Enables pre-screening questions before outreach sends. Your recruiter reviews and approves — LiHA doesn't act without a human in the loop. The drafting, personalizing, and scheduling? Done.
Step 4
Evaluate Applicants AI
Triages applicants from LinkedIn and your ATS into top / maybe / not a fit — with the reasoning shown. Your recruiter stops wading through 200 applications. They review the 20 that matter.
Step 5
Relationships, Decisions, Closing Your Recruiter
This is where your team lives. The real conversations, the sell, the culture read, the close. LiHA gives 20+ hours a week back per recruiter — not by replacing them, but by eliminating everything that isn't this.
